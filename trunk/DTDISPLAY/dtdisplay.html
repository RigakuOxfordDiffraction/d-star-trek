dtdisplay on-line help

On-line help in dtdisplay is provided by HyperText Markup Language files that are viewable via a HTML viewer such as NCSA mosaic. Help is available by choosing Help in the dtdisplay menubar. With dtdisplay you can:

  • Display d*TREK images in any of eight orientations with a variety of color scales,
  • Zoom, Pan and Resize the image display,
  • Pick spots and display pixel value, resolution, integrated intensity and background average,
  • Measure the d-spacing between selected spots,
  • Display saturated pixels in selected colors,
  • Tile a series of images,
  • Overlay a series of images,
  • Display images with non-square pixels in true aspect ratio,
  • Display images as they are collected by dtcollect.

For more help, choose one of the following topics:

Hyperlinks are usually underlined and displayed in blue letters.

To close a Help window, choose File->Close or File->Exit from the menubar in this window.
